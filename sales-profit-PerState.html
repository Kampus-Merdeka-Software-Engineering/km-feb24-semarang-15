    Profit Per State

    Based on the data on the geographic map above, the western region shows that over the last five years, the City of California has experienced a significant increase in profits, followed by the City of Washington which is ranked second. Although the City of Washington experienced a decrease in profits in 2016, it experienced an increase in the following years. In the eastern region, New York recorded the highest profits compared to any other state, showing strong profit performance over the past five years. Meanwhile, Pennsylvania showed the largest loss, much larger than Ohio. In the central region, Michigan recorded the largest gains among all the states mentioned, showing very strong economic strength over the 5-year period (2014-2017). However, Illinois and Texas were the only states to experience losses, with Texas experiencing particularly large losses over a 5-year period (2014-2017). Finally, in the south, the cities of Virginia, Georgia, and Kentucky have had the largest gains among all these states, indicating very strong and stable economies over the past 5 years. Other states also showed significant gains, indicating a positive trend in economic growth, except Tennessee which experienced losses.
