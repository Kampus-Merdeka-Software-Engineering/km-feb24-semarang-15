Team 15 Section Semarang

Team 15 from the Semarang section is a dynamic ensemble of twelve aspiring individuals, deeply immersed in the realms of data analysis and software engineering through their journey at RevoU Tech Academy. With a shared passion for harnessing the power of data and code, this team embodies collaboration and innovation. Each member brings a unique set of skills and perspectives to the table, enriching the collective expertise of the group. Their dedication to mastering the intricacies of modern technology is evident in their unwavering commitment to excellence and their relentless pursuit of knowledge. As they navigate through challenges and triumphs together, Team 15 stands as a testament to the transformative potential of teamwork and perseverance in the ever-evolving landscape of tech education.
