    Profit and Sales Per Year

    The "Profit and Sales" line graph displays trends in both profit and sales from January 2014 to October 2017. The orange profit line has substantial peaks and troughs, with the latter part of 2017 showing very strong fluctuations. Its mean is not much higher than 40M. The green sales line shows some stability but also varies, it falls below zero in early 2014 and mid-2015, then starts to rise in late 2016. It averages out to be about 20M. In contrast to the more regular sales, profit exhibits sharper spikes and greater unpredictability. Profit averages are constantly higher, suggesting fluctuating profit margins even in the presence of stable sales. Overall, the graph shows a dynamic link between sales and profit, with profit being generally higher and more unpredictable than sales.
